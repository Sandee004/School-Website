National Curriculum We Run

Jephthah runs a sound national curriculum throughout all classes: JSS1-3 as well as from SS1-3

The school is a centre for the West African Senior School Certificate examinations(WASSCE), NECO, BECE and SSCE. The examination is compulsory for all grade 12 students in Jephthah.

Jephthah Comprehensive Secondary School is approved by the Rivers State Ministry of Education to run both the Junior and Senior Secondary Schools. We are approved as a centre for the Rivers State JSCE and the examination is compulsory for all Junior Secondary (JS3) grade 9 students.

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) is a Nigerian entrance examination board for tertiary level institutions. The board conducts entrance examinations for prospective undergraduates into Nigerian universities through the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME). The exam is compulsory for all grade 12 students in Jephthah.
